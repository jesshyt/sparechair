Christmas campaign



The 25th of December is a day meant for eating, drinking and celebrating with friends and loved ones alike. But sadly, this is not the case for everyone and many people around the country are due to spend Christmas day alone. We need your help to combat loneliness on the most special day of the year – and you can even help us in our quest from the comfort of your own home!

Wherever you may be in the country, Dunstable to Dumfries, Southampton to Solihull, you can lend us a hand (or maybe a chair!) in any of these three ways:

1) Volunteer at a local Spare Chair Christmas Kitchen…

On 25th December, we have organised over 100 special kitchen and dining facilities up and down the country to provide food and home comforts to those who would otherwise be spending Christmas alone. We are in need of some extra pairs of hands in the kitchen, but don’t worry; you won’t miss out – there’ll be a ‘Spare Chair’ at the table for you too!

2) Make use of the ‘Spare Chair’ at your dining table…

Our prime objective is not taking a break over the Christmas period – in fact, quite the opposite! We are working tirelessly to ensure that no seat is left unfilled on the 25th December around the country. So if you have a chair to spare around your dinner table this Christmas day, please do get in touch and we will work alongside you to match you with those lonely and in need in your local area.

3) Sign up or sign someone up!

Let us know if you have someone in mind that is due to be spending Christmas alone that you think we can help – or likewise, let us know if that person is you!


Whichever option you choose, please use the sign up form below and we will get back in touch with you with further details. Let’s eat and be merry!
